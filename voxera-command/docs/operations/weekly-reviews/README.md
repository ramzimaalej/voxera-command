# Weekly business reviews

One file per week. Filename: `YYYY-MM-DD.md` (the Friday or week-ending date). Generated by the `weekly-business-review` babysitter process; archived here.

## Why this exists

The single most valuable rhythm for a bootstrapped founder is a weekly review that:
- Forces you to face the numbers and the customer state, not just feel them.
- Holds last week's commitments accountable to what actually happened.
- Sets the next week's top 3 priorities while you have full state in mind.

Skipping a week is the leading indicator of operational drift.
