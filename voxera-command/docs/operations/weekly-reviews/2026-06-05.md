## Surprises
- **We're losing money at today's run-rate.** 4 closed/day × 40% = 1.6 confirmed/day → ~€3.2k/mo revenue against ~€10k/mo cost. Break-even needs **12.5 closed/day** (3.1× today).
- **It's conversion, not calling time.** Reps already dial 200/250 calls/day (~80%); the problem is ~10% lead→close (1% call→close). Tripling conversion ≈ break-even with the current 2 reps.
- **700 deals dead — and locked.** The backlog is reactivatable only after a 3-month cold period, so it can't relieve lead spend now. The AI over-generates (~50/day) vs what reps can work (~40/day), which is what built the pile.

## Blockers
- **~3 weeks of runway.** €0 reserves; $5k credit headroom vs ~€7.07k/mo burn. Break-even is unreachable by sales in that window, so the survival move is to **secure cash / extend credit / cut the €5,060/mo AI call spend now**. Owner: you — this is the #1 item.
- **Low sales conversion** is the path back to break-even: 4 closed/day vs **12.5 needed**; the lever is lead→close (10% → ~30%), not more dialing. Slower than the runway — run both tracks in parallel.
- **Concentration risk**: Customer XY is now 100% of revenue. One contract = the whole business until a second account is added.
